                          ABOUT ICE HEALTH CHECK FRAMEWORK
                          ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++


    The health check framework is being developed by the ICE SRE group.

    The Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) group is focused on solving critical problems in Nokia CBIS and NCS products and
    developing tools for creating an efficient and automatic troubleshooting process.

    The health check framework aims to reduce the investigation time of the root cause by detection of known issues in the system,
    wrong configurations, lack of resources and much more information that can help in the problem resolution.

    At the end of the health check running, output files are generated in several formats, for providing the result in the preferred way.

    More infomation can be found at https://nokia.sharepoint.com/sites/CloudBand-ICE/SitePages/SRE-Support-Package-%26-Tools.aspx#ice-health-check-support-tools
